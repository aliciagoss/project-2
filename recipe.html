Choclate Pudding

This chocolate pudding recipe takes no time at all on the stovetop, and then a quick chill while you're eating dinner and voila!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cup cocoa
  • 2/3 cup cornstarch
  • pinch salt
  • 4 1/2 cups milk
  • 3-4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions
